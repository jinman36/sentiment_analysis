Army Work

I joined the Army in 2005 as a Parachute Rigger. From there, I was stationed at Fort Campbell, KY and worked with 160 SOAR(A). I worked there until 2011, when I moved to the USASOC Parachute Team, the Black Daggers. While there I made close to 1,100 jumps from aircraft, was on ESPN jumping into stadiums and even got to give some outstanding Marines thier first tandem jumps (see the link below)

Marine Tandem Article
